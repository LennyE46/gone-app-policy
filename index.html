GoNE App Account and Data Deletion

Last Updated: June 7, 2025

You have the right to request the deletion of your GoNE app account and all associated data.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Your account in our authentication system (including your email and nickname).
  • All of your RSVP records for events ("Going," "Maybe," or "Not Going").

How to Request Deletion:

To request the deletion of your account, please send an email from the address associated with your GoNE account to: goneapp2025@gmail.com

Please use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

We will process your request and confirm via email once your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted from our servers. Please note that this action is irreversible.
